You are given an input box, where you will enter numbers between 0 and 20. If your number matched the generated number in 20 tries then you 1 point is added into you HighScore. Everytime your number matched 1 point will be added to your Highscore. But if, you are unable to match the number everytime your tries will get minus 1 and if your number haven't matched in 20 tries, then u would lost the game.
